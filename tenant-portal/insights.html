Insights

The Insights page gives valuable overview information to help identify problem areas that need addressing

These insights can show:

  • High Vehicle Incidents
  • High Incident Period
  • Week Day With High Incidents
  • An active vehicle doesn’t have any red alarms
  • An active vehicle doesn’t have any amber alarms
  • A vehicle has a high ratio of red to amber alarms
  • A vehicle has a low number of alarms on one particular camera
  • A site has a significantly higher number of alarms compared to previous period

This is not an exhaustive list.

Options are available within each insight.

TAKE ACTION

  • NOT CURRENTLY ACTIVE

REMOVE

Clicking on the cross will remove that insight from the list.
