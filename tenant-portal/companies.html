Companies

The Companies tab in the Settings page details the current companies of the tenant, along with the ability to add new companies and edit existing companies

Operations that can be performed

  • Add Company
  • Change Hierarchy
  • Edit existing company
  • Remove a company
  • Change the hierarchy of a company

Adding a Company

To add a company, click on the ADD COMPANY button. This will open a new window where you can enter the details of the new company.

Step 1 - Enter the company name

Step 2 - Enter the company address

Step 3 - Enter the company description

Step 4 - Enable the company in the portal

Step 5 - Select if sub company data whould be surfaced to the parent

Step 6 - Select the teams that can access this company

Step 7 - Click on the SAVE button to save the company

Change Hierarchy

Clicking on the CHANGE HIERARCHY button. This will open a new window where you can edit the hierarchy of the company.

You can assign the hierarchy of the companies, by clicking and dragging, a company to the desired position

Clicking the SAVE button will save the changes made, whilst the CANCEL button will disregard changes made and return to the companies list.

Companies Table

The companies table details all the companies that have been created in the portal.

Operations that can be performed

  • Edit the company
  • Remove the company
  • Select the active company

Each row gives the following details

  • Title
    • Name of the company
  • Address
    • Address of the company
  • Enabled
    • The enabled status of the company, within the portal
  • Actions
    • Edit - Edit the company
    • Remove - Remove the company
  • Active Company
    • Set the company as the active company

Editing a Company

To edit a company, click on the PENCIL icon. This will open a new window where you can edit the details of the company.

All of the fields can then be edited as required.

Clicking on the SAVE button will save the changes, whilst the CANCEL button will disregard any changes and return to the company list

Removing a Company

To remove a company, click on the TRASH button. This will open a new window where you can confirm the removal of the company.

Clicking on the DELETE button will remove the company, whilst the CANCEL button will disregard the removal and return to the company list

Setting the Active Company

To set the active company, click on the SELECT button. This will set the company as the active company.

Pagination

Several options are available to move through the list of active invitations

  • Selecting the number of users to show, via the drop-down

  • Using the pagination navigation controls
