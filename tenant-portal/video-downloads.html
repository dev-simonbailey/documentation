Video Downloads

The Video Downloads page details all the incident videos that have been requested.

Filtering

You can filter the data by the following:

Vehicle registration

Multiple vehicles may be selected. Allow searching by license plate.

Video Downloads Table

The video downloads table shows each incident video download request details, along the actions that are available

The details are:

  • Requested by
    • User who requested this video
  • Status
    • None
      • The incident video download request has not been approved or declined
    • Green Thumbs Up
      • The incident video download request has been approved
    • Red Thumbs Down
      • The incident video download request has been declined
  • Incident occurred at
    • Date and time the requested incident occurred at
  • Vehicle Registration
    • The registration of the vehicle the incident occurred with
  • Machine Status
    • The status of the device
      • Green is online
      • Red is offline
  • Actions
    • Flag
      • Toggle between flagged and unflagged
    • Notes
      • Notes assigned to the request
        • Clicking on the Note icon will display the note entry modal

  • PLAY
    • Play the incident video
  • REPLAY
    • If the current user has watched the video, the replay icon is shown, allowing them to replay the video.
  • TICK
    • Approve the download request
  • CROSS
    • Decline the download request

Pagination

Several options are available to move through the list of video downloads

  • Selecting the number of video downloads to show, via the drop-down

  • Using the pagination navigation controls
