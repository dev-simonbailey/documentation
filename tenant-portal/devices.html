Devices

The Devices Tab, within the Settings page is divided into two sections

Assigned Devices

Unssigned Devices

Assigned Devices Table

Each row of the table details devices that are assigned to vehicles

  • UUID
    • The UUID of the device
  • Vehicle
    • The vehicle the device is assigned to
  • First Online
    • The date and time the device was first online
  • Last Online
    • The date and time the device was last online
  • Expires
    • The date and time the device authorisation expires
  • Enabled
    • The device is enabled in the portals
  • Visible
    • The device is visible
  • Deadman
    • Record if the deadman lever is activated
  • Device Issues
    • Issues the device is currently experiencing
  • Actions
    • Access Live Camera Feed
    • Edit the device details

Any details highlighted in red are for devices that have reached or passed their expiry date

No data is provided for devices that have expired

If there are any devices that have reached or passed their expiry date, then a banner will be visible at the top of the page

Actions

Access Live Camera Feed

  • Access Live Camera Feed by clicking the CAMERA icon

  • When clicked, it will then display the live feed modal, and clicking on any of the available cameras will initiate the live feed stream from the vehicle.

Edit the device details

  • Edit the device details by clicking the PENCIL icon

Operations that can be performed

  • Adding or editing the note attached to the device
  • Assign the device to a vehicle
  • Enabling the device
  • Making the device visible on the portal
  • Enabling deadman

Clicking on the SAVE button will update the device details Clicking on the CANCEL button will cancel the editing operation

Unassigned Devices

The Unassigned Devices Table details devices that are not assigned to any vehicle

The details and operations of Unassigned Devices is the same as Asssigned devices, so please refer to the sections above for further details.

Filtering

Devices can be filtered by selecting the desired filter from the drop-down menu

Pagination

Several options are available to move through the list of devices

  • Selecting the number of devices to show, via the drop down

  • Using the pagination navigation controls
