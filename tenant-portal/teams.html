Teams

The Teams tab in the Settings page details the current teams of the portal, along with the ability to add new users and edit existing users

Operations that can be performed

  • Add Team
  • Edit team

Adding a team

Clicking the ADD TEAM button will display the Add Team page

Step 1 - Enter the team name

Step 2 - Create the team

Team Table

The team table details the following

  • Name
  • Owner
  • Actions
    • Edit

Editing a team

Clicking the PENCIL icon will display the Edit Team page, which comprises of 8 sections

Team Name

Here the name of the team can be edited

Add Team Member

Here you can add team members by entering their email address

Team Permissions

In this section, the permissions that members of this team are granted can be selected

Company Access

In this section, the companies that members of this team are granted can be selected

Fleets Access

In this section, the fleets that members of this team are granted access to, can be selected

Sites Access

In this section, the sites that members of this team are granted access to, can be selected

Vehicles Access

In this section, the vehicles that members of this team are granted access to, can be selected

Delete Team

In this section, the selected team can be deleted.

Once a team is deleted, all of its resources and data will be permanently deleted. Before deleting this team, please download any data or information regarding this team that you wish to retain.
